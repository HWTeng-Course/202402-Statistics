## Solution


$S_{xx} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} (x_i - \bar{x})(x_i - \bar{x})=1.58113883$\
$S_{xy} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} (x_i - \bar{x})(y_i - \bar{y})=3$

$y=a+bx$\
$b=\frac{S_{xy}}{S_{xx}}=1.2$\
$\bar{y}$與 $\bar{x}$ 代入得到 $a$\
$a=3$\
得到 $y=3+1.2x$
