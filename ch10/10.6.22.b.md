### Solution
degree of freedom :\
$v_1 = n_1 - 1 = 15 -1 = 14$\
$v_2 = n_2 - 1 = 15 -1 = 14$

99% confidence interval for $\dfrac{\sigma_1^2}{\sigma_2^2}$ :\
[ $\dfrac{s_1^2}{s_2^2}$ $\dfrac{1}{F_{v_1,v_2;\alpha/2}}$ , $\dfrac{s_1^2}{s_2^2}$ $F_{v_1,v_2;\alpha/2}$ ]
