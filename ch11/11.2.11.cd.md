## Solution
### c
$H_0: μ1 - μ4 = 0$,

$H_1: μ1 - μ4 ≠ 0$

α = 0.05

Test statistic: 
$\ t = \frac{{\bar{x}_1 - \bar{x}_4}}{{\sqrt{\frac{{s_1^2}}{{n_1}} + \frac{{s_4^2}}{{n_4}}}}} \$
